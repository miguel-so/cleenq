CleenQ Domestic Cleaning
Enjoy a spotless home with CleenQ’s professional domestic cleaning services. We offer flexible options for regular upkeep, deep cleaning, or end-of-lease cleaning — all carried out by trained and insured professionals.
Standard Clean
What’s Included:
• Dusting of all reachable surfaces
• Vacuum and mop all floors
• Kitchen clean — benches, sinks, stovetops, splashbacks
• Bathroom clean — shower, toilet, mirror, sink
• Light tidy-up and rubbish removal
• Cobweb removal and spot cleaning where required
Deep Clean
What’s Included:
• Everything in Standard Clean, plus:
• Detailed skirting board and door cleaning
• Window tracks and internal glass wiped
• Blinds dusted and light fittings cleaned
• Deep bathroom and kitchen degrease
• Wall spot cleaning (where accessible)
End of Lease Clean
What’s Included:
• Everything in Deep Clean, plus:
• Inside oven, rangehood, and stovetop detailing
• Inside cupboards, drawers, and wardrobes cleaned
• Windows and tracks (interior) cleaned
• Bathrooms sanitised and descaled
• Floors and skirting boards thoroughly washed
• Final inspection guarantee (clean until it passes inspection)
Notes
• All prices include GST and cleaning products.
• Additional charges may apply for heavily soiled areas or large properties.
• Optional extras: fridge, oven, blinds, balconies, and exterior windows.
• Discounts available for regular cleaning schedules.
• ‘Where Quality Meets Clean’ — every time.
