CleenQ Cleaning App – Full Scope Document
This document outlines the full scope for the CleenQ Cleaning App, a nationwide cleaning service platform designed to manage client bookings, cleaner scheduling, loyalty programs, and integration with Servis Software. The app will be developed for both iOS and Android platforms, with a responsive web portal for administration and client access.
1. Project Overview
CleenQ aims to deliver a modern, simple, and reliable cleaning experience through an all-in-one mobile and web application. The platform allows customers to instantly quote, book, and pay for cleaning services online, while cleaners and managers coordinate through a dedicated mobile interface.
2. Core Objectives
• Simplify customer booking and quoting processes.
• Streamline cleaner scheduling and job management.
• Integrate with Servis Software for quote, booking, and scheduling synchronization.
• Implement a loyalty and rewards program to retain customers.
• Support all 9 CleenQ cleaning services across Australia.
3. App Modules
3.1 Client App (iOS, Android, Web)
The Client App will allow users to:
• View, quote, and book services instantly.
• Choose service type, date/time, and additional add-ons.
• Make secure payments through Stripe or PayPal.
• Track cleaner arrival and job progress in real time.
• Earn and redeem loyalty points ($1 = 1 point; 500 points = $25 off).
• Manage subscription/membership tiers (Basic, Premium, Platinum).
• Access booking history, invoices, and receipts.
• Submit ratings and feedback.
• Receive push notifications for job status, rewards, and promotions.
3.2 Cleaner App (iOS, Android)
The Cleaner App will allow CleenQ cleaners and contractors to:
• View assigned jobs and upcoming schedule.
• Accept or decline jobs (based on availability).
• Access job details, location, and client instructions.
• Complete checklists with photos and notes.
• Log start/finish times and generate automatic time sheets.
• Upload before/after images and customer signatures.
• Sync data automatically with Servis Software.
• View payments, completed jobs, and ratings.
• Access support, training, and policy documents.
3.3 Admin & Management Portal (Web Dashboard)
The Admin Portal will enable CleenQ management to:
• Oversee all bookings, jobs, clients, and cleaners.
• Manage quotes, invoices, and payments via Servis Software integration.
• View live calendar of scheduled jobs.
• Assign or reassign cleaners manually.
• Track job completion, ratings, and cleaner performance.
• Monitor revenue, bookings, and client retention analytics.
• Manage loyalty and promotional campaigns.
• Send notifications and newsletters to clients.
• Export data and generate reports (daily, weekly, monthly).
4. Integration with Servis Software
The CleenQ App will integrate directly with Servis Software via API or webhook for the following functionalities:
• Quote synchronization – automatically create and update quotes.
• Booking synchronization – reflect confirmed jobs from Servis in CleenQ calendar.
• Client sync – share client details and booking history.
• Schedule synchronization – match job timings between Servis and CleenQ.
• Invoice generation and payment recording.
5. Loyalty & Rewards Program
The CleenQ Rewards System will allow customers to earn and redeem points for services.
• 1 point for every $1 spent.
• Redeem 500 points for $25 discount.
• Tier levels:
  - Basic: 0–999 points – Standard pricing.
  - Premium: 1,000–2,999 points – 5% discount on all services.
  - Platinum: 3,000+ points – 10% discount and priority booking.
• Integration with Stripe for automated points tracking.
• Points visible in the app dashboard and checkout.

7. Technical Specifications
• Platforms: iOS, Android, Web (React / React Native preferred).
• Backend: Node.js / Python Flask with Servis API integration.
• Database: PostgreSQL or Firebase.
• Payment Gateway: Stripe / PayPal.
• Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging / OneSignal.
• Hosting: AWS / Google Cloud.
• Security: SSL encryption, role-based access, GDPR-compliant storage.
8. UI/UX Design
• Mobile-first, clean design matching CleenQ brand (“Where Quality Meets Clean”).
• Booking flow optimized for <60 seconds completion.
• Branded colour palette and typography.
• Customer-friendly calendar and pricing view.
• Intuitive cleaner dashboard with checklist-based navigation.
9. Deliverables
